Services We Provide

Root Canal Treatment

Root canal treatment is the removal of the infected soft tissue with in the tooth and replacing it with an artificial inert filling material. This procedure saves the tooth and gives complete relief from the dental pain.



Dental Implant

A dental implant is an artificial replacement for a tooth, usually made from titanium.
It is the surgical component that connects with the bone of the jaws or skull bone to support a dental prosthesis such as a bridge, crown, denture, facial prosthesis or to act as an orthodontic anchor.



Invisible Braces

Invisalign is a treatment option that relies on transparent retainer like aligners to shift teeth into place.
There are a great option for people who went to straighten their teeth while avoiding a metal mouth smile.



Smile Design and Aesthetics

Creating a pleasing teeth arrangement in harmony with the gingiva, lips and face.



Full mouth rehabilitation

Theraphy for Temperomandibular Joint Disorders

Temporomandibular joint and muscle disorders, commonly called “TMJ”, are a group of conditions that cause pain and dysfunction in the jaw joint and the muscles that control jaw movements.



Oral Cancer and Potenially Maligant Disorders

Oral potentially malignant disorders include a variety of lesions and conditions characterized by an increase risk for malignant transformation to oral squamous cell carcinoma.
